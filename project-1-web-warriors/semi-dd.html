Stetson Eye Movement and Individual Differences Dataset (SEMI-DD)

Sabbatical project containing a data corpus from more than 200 participants who read 55 passages (2700+ words each). The corpus includes 30+ measures of eye movement behavior, 20+ variables of lexical characteristics, and 60+ measures of reading skill of each participant.
